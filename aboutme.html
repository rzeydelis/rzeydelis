About Me

I graduated from Binghamton University with a degree in Economics. Starting out in fintech, I later joined Mu Sigma - a data analytics company - as a consultant. Data analytics is a very interesting field because of how it turns decision making from just being an art to being a science and an art.

Outside of work, I love to read, excercise, travel and try new things. I read everything from biographies to analytics books to fictions. You can check out my reading list on this website. In my spare time, I love to go to the gym, play basketball, swim and relax in a sauna. Sauna is a great way to relax the muscles after a long week. I have lived in Bangalore, India for 6 months for Mu Sigma and have travelled around Asia. I have been to Nepal, Thailand, Tokyo and Hong Kong during my stay in India. I also visited Goa, Bangalore, Kodaikanal and Pondicherry while in India. I recommend spending some time in Asia if you are adventurous while it is still cheap.
